Site Plan: Local Coffee Shop

Site Name

Bright Haven

This name evokes a warm and cozy atmosphere where people can enjoy a quality brew and find a relaxing haven. It's short, memorable, and fits the branding of a local coffee shop.

Optional domain availability: brighthavencoffee.shop

Site Purpose

This website provides information about Bright Haven Coffee Shop, including the drinks and snacks menu, shop history, and contact information. It aims to attract new visitors, provide essential details like the address and hours, and engage the community with a welcoming online presence.

Scenarios

  • What drinks and snacks are available at Bright Haven?
  • How can I contact Bright Haven or find their location?

Color Scheme

  • #6b4226 - Rich Coffee Brown (used for headings and accents)
  • #fef9f4 - Light Cream (used for the background)

This warm, inviting palette reflects the ambiance of a cozy coffee shop and keeps the design clean and pleasant for reading.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – for main headings to give a warm, classic café vibe.
  • Roboto – for body text and menus, for clean readability and modern contrast.

Wireframe

Mobile View

Simple stacked layout: logo, hero image, brief intro, navigation, menu preview, and footer.

Desktop View

Header with logo and navigation bar, hero image beside text intro, menu grid, about section, map/contact form, and footer.
